The LEADer Grant is supported by Project LEAD, if you're interested in supporting this project contact us!


The LEADer Grant was created in order to allow students to work on projects that they otherise wouldn't be able to.

Project LEAD Offers it's support during all stages of the process and provides feedback to all applicants regardless if they win the grant.

In exchange for the grant, recipients are required to do a write-up of their project and make an informative video for the Project LEAD YouTube page
